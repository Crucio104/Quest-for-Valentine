A charming pixel-art adventure game created for Valentine's Day. Navigate through a mystical forest, solve riddles, and make choices to find your Valentine (or perhaps... something else?). This game features a dynamic narrative, interactive dialogue, and a robust control system.

## ‚ú® Features

*   **Branching Narrative:** Your choices matter! Discover 10 unique endings, from romantic successes to hilarious failures.
*   **Pixel Art Aesthetic:** Immerse yourself in a retro-styled world with custom pixel art backgrounds, UI elements, and a dynamic particle system.
*   **Audio & Atmosphere:**
    *   **Adaptive Soundtrack:** Music changes based on the scene (Romantic, Mysterious, Danger).
    *   **Procedural Ambience:** Real-time generated environmental sounds (Forest, River, Cave).
    *   **Sound Effects:** Engaging typing sounds, button clicks, and achievement notifications.
*   **Advanced Controls & Accessibility:**
    *   **Keyboard Navigation:** Use Arrow Keys, Enter, and Space to navigate dialogue and menus.
    *   **Control Panel:** Easily toggle Fullscreen, Mute Audio, View Achievements, and Adjust Settings.
    *   **Volume Control:** Precise master volume slider in Settings.
*   **Progress Tracking:**
    *   **Save System:** Your progress (current scene) is automatically saved.
    *   **Achievements:** Unlock and track special achievements, including the "Valentine Master" badge for finding all endings.

## üöÄ How to Play

1.  **Start:** Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
2.  **Begin:** Click anywhere or press Space/Enter on the title screen.
3.  **Read:** Follow the story as text appears.
4.  **Choose:** Select an option to progress the story. Your decisions will lead you down different paths!
5.  **Manage:** Use the control panel (bottom right) to adjust settings or view your achievements.
6.  **Unlock:** Try to find all 10 endings to complete your achievement collection!
